Resume & Skills Helper

Get your application ready

Free, human resume help is available across Orange County — you don't need to build a resume alone.

Before you go, bring:

  • A list of past jobs, dates, and what you did in each role
  • Any certificates, licenses, or training you've completed
  • Two or three references with phone numbers, if you have them
  • A valid email address you check regularly
